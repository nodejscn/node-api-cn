* {string}

The type of the performance entry. It may be one of:

* `'node'` (Node.js only)
* `'mark'` (available on the Web)
* `'measure'` (available on the Web)
* `'gc'` (Node.js only)
* `'function'` (Node.js only)
* `'http2'` (Node.js only)
* `'http'` (Node.js only)
